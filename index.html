IF window · 12:00–19:00
12:00 — break fast (pre-workout)
First meal + fuel
60–90 min before training. 40–50g protein + 60–80g carbs. Eggs + rice + chicken, or Greek yogurt + banana + protein shake. Most important meal of the day.
During (if over 60 min)
Intra-workout
500ml water + 5g creatine monohydrate (take daily regardless). Keep it simple.
Within 60 min of finishing
Post-workout
40–50g protein + 50–60g fast carbs. Shake + banana, or rice + lean protein. Must happen before 19:00.
15:00–18:00
Main meal
40g protein, complex carbs, veg, healthy fats. Salmon + sweet potato + greens, or beef + rice + salad.
Before 19:00
Optional snack
Cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, or casein shake. Slow protein before the fast window supports overnight repair.
Key tips
Creatine monohydrate 5g daily — most evidence-backed supplement for strength. Timing doesn't matter, consistency does.
Protein first at every meal. If a meal doesn't have 30g+, it won't support your goals.
2.5–3L water daily, +500ml on training days. Dehydration kills strength by up to 10%.
Rest days: same protein, cut carbs ~30%. Don't restrict fat — it supports testosterone at 45.
7–8 hours sleep. At 45, growth hormone release during sleep is your primary muscle-building window. Non-negotiable.

Progression rules
Weekly add
+2.5kg barbell, +1–2kg dumbbells — only if all reps completed with good form.
Failed reps
Repeat same weight next week. Two failures → drop 5% and rebuild.
Pull-ups
Reduce assist 2.5kg/week. At zero → full pull-ups in Month 2.
Squat singles
Skip Week 1. Introduce Week 2 once activation set feels locked in.
Week 5
Deload — all weights –20%, same structure. Export history and send to Claude for Month 2.
